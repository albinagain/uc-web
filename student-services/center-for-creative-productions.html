Center for Creative Productions

About

The University of the Cordilleras (UC) Center for Creative Productions (CCP) is the Performing Arts Center of the University that catalyzes performances either on-campus productions or official representations for off-campus events. The Center was established on January 8, 2008 unifying the different events. Since then, the Center has produced world-class performances garnering numerous awards in performing arts.

UC Dance Company (UCDC)

The UC Dance Company is a competitive and contemporary dance group of UC-CCP. Formerly known as UC Dance Squad, the dance group has won and has received numerous awards nationally. The squad was renamed UC Dance Company (UCDC) in 2016 to accommodate the diverse talents coming from the Senior High School Department. Presently, the UCDC is one of the leading dance groups in the Philippines.

UC Saeng Ya Kasay Cultural Ensemble

The UC Saeng Ya Kasay Cultural Ensemble is a specialized performing group that present distinct music, never-dying rituals, and cultural dances from the Mountains of the Cordillera. These are presented through full or one-act theatrical renditions where performers are fully dressed in authentic indigenous attires and accessories supported by aboriginal instruments.

UC Chorale

The UC Chorale is the official vocal ensemble of the University that performs an array of choral music styles and forms such as local and international Folk Songs, Sacred Music, Popular/ Pop, Jazz, Broadway, Classical, and Contemporary.

UC Percussion and Orchestra

The UC Percussion and Orchestra is a musical ensemble that has sections on strings, brass and percussion, drums, group band, and newly formed djembe. Anon, the group has been considered as the premier-performing musical group and the first orchestra band in Baguio City.
