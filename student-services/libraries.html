Libraries

Library Orientation

This program is designed to acquaint the students and employees with the support the library can provide. It introduces them to the UC Libraries and its services, resources, and policies. Thus, educating them on the general use of the library.

Library Search Assistance

This service provides an overview of the library e-resources/databases including instructional guides/tutorials on how to access these resources. This will help the patrons use the resources efficiently and effectively.

Bookwatch

A library bulletin highlighting the newly acquired library materials. It may be produced either in print or as electronic copy for posting in the Learning Management System or attachment in e-mail.

Featured Books

A library bulletin featuring selected references. This is posted in the Learning Management System.

Book Display

An exhibit of theme-based or newly acquired books.

Book List

A list of subject references per academic program. This is provided as per request by the patrons.

Online Document Delivery

  1. Patrons may request an e-copy of the topic which may be scanned from available library collection such as journal articles, book chapters/parts, and research/thesis abstracts; or a download of e-books and e-journal articles, subject to the Fair Use Policy. Delivery of requested scanned/downloaded documents is through email.
  2. The average turnaround time to complete the processing of request is within two (2) school days. Counting starts after the requested date.

Borrowing and Returning

On-site Borrowing

Patrons who are allowed for campus entry/face-to-face classes may borrow a library material on-site.

Online Borrowing of Books

  1. Patrons may borrow a book by requesting online and pick it up at the UC Libraries.
  2. The average turnaround time to complete the processing of request is within two (2) school days. Counting starts after the requested date.
  • College
  • SHS

Renewal

  1. Patrons may request for a renewal of a borrowed library material.
  2. Renewal is granted if the library material is not requested by another patron, or the copy is not busy for the meantime, or there are other available copies.
  3. Request may be done through phone, SMS, email, or in person at the Circulation Desk.
  4. A new due date is calculated based on the day the library material is renewed.

Book Drop

  1. Patrons may drop borrowed books in the library’s Book Drop (available 24/7) provided at UC Main Campus Gate 1 or near Security Post at Legarda Campus and Campo Libertad.
  2. A confirmation will be sent via e-mail to acknowledge the return of library materials.

Centralized Circulation Desk

Patrons may give the borrowed library materials to the library personnel at the Centralized Circulation Area, 5F Main Library (Monday to Saturday, 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.).

Library Notification

Patrons may be notified through email of their library accountabilities:

  1. recall/reminder on borrowed library materials,
  2. acknowledgement of book returns, and
  3. signed library clearance.

Library Hours

8:00 am – 5:00 pm
Mondays to Saturdays except on holidays and class suspensions

Library Announcement

Patrons are informed of library advisories/updates on events/activities, services, resources, and policies through bulletins and online platforms.
