Office of the Student Welfare Services

“Seeing your Inner Potential, Extending a Helping Hand, and Listening with a Heart to bring out the Jaguar in you”

Vision

The Integrated School Student Welfare Services Office envisions itself as a nationally recognized complementary unit for the holistic development of individuals who are able to face the challenges of everyday life.

Mission

The Integrated School Student Welfare Services Office delivers exceptional programs characterized by innovative, developmental, and dynamic service to its stakeholders.

Objectives

The Integrated School Student Welfare Services Office delivers exceptional programs characterized by innovative, developmental, and dynamic service to its stakeholders.

  • Implement innovative, developmental and dynamic programs for holistic development of learners.
  • Establish mutually beneficial collaboration with stakeholders for effective program implementation.
  • Establish an office that designs exceptional programs that are implemented by highly competent Guidance Counselors.

Services Delivered

Information Service

Provides information on the five pillars of holistic development for stakeholders.

  • Personal and physical growth
  • Social Skills
  • Emotional Understanding
  • Intellectual development
  • Environmental values

Online/Offline Kumustahan Session (O.K.S.)

Conducts one-on-one or Group Session regarding learner’s concern to assist them in dealing with challenges in their daily lives.

Career Orientation

Provides individuals necessary information and experience to prepare them for living and working in an ever- changing economy, society and environment

Consultation

Clarifies queries and processes concerns of students, parents, and teachers through various platforms.

Referral

Refers learners to concerned office or agency whose concerns are beyond the competency of the SWF.

P.E.E.R.S Affiliation

Reaches learners by Priming, Enriching, and Empowering through skills development and academic assistance.
