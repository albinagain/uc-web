Grade School

About

The University of the Cordilleras Integrated School Grade School is a non-stock, non-profit educational institution. A PACUCOA ACCREDITED (LEVEL II) academic community and ISO 9001:2015 certified. The school believes in holistic scholastic development of its learners and that education should be accessible to all regardless of race, economic standing, or religion. The Kindergarten facilitates personality growth and development of children. The Grade School provides fun and engaging learning through active instructional approach.

Vision

The UC-IS Grade School envisions itself as a premier elementary school for the formation of young learners equipped with desired competencies and imbued with noble sense of values.
